ETHNIC DAY

India is home to various traditions and diverse cultures. We follow the theme of unity in diversity which allows every Indian to be exposed to other cultures. But at present, We Indians are becoming unaware of the cultures and traditions followed by our previous generations. But at present most of us are unaware of many traditions passed down by our ancestors.
In order to let everyone know of our cultures and Traditions, Our EBSB club conducted ethnic day on November 22,2019. On this day our students wore traditional dresses which symbolised their nativity.

VISIT TO GONGLUR VILLAGE

EBSB group of IIT-H conducted a visit to a nearby village, Gongloor, Sangareddy district.
Here our students took part in programmes, which focused on transforming the village into a Model Village. Our students analysed the administration of the village and proposed some innovative steps and took part in vidyadaan programme in the government school. We also taught villagers the importance of SWACHH BHARAT, and made them aware of some state of art agricultural practices. We also helped in cleaning the roads of the village. We planned this visit in order to help villages progress which in turn develops the nation.
